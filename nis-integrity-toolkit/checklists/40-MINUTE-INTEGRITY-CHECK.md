## üìã PHASE 1: DOCUMENTATION REALITY CHECK (15 minutes)

### üéØ **README.md Verification** (5 minutes)

- [ ] **Scope Clarity**: Clearly states what the system does AND doesn't do

### üìä **Performance Claims Audit** (5 minutes)

### üèóÔ∏è **Architecture Alignment** (5 minutes)
